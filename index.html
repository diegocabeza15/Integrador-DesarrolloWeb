    About Us

    "At Bean Machine, we're passionate about coffee and freedom. Founded in 2010 in the city of Los Santos, our mission is to provide our customers with the best coffee experience while promoting a lifestyle without limits. Our roots go back to the streets of Los Santos, where our founder, a coffee enthusiast and speed lover, decided to combine his two passions to create a unique brand. Since then, we've grown rapidly, expanding across the city and becoming a symbol of Los Santos culture. At Bean Machine, we believe in quality, innovation, and freedom. Our expert baristas are committed to excellence, and our development team works tirelessly to create new and exciting experiences for our customers. Whether you're looking for a coffee to go on your next drive, or a place to relax and enjoy a moment of tranquility, Bean Machine is your destination. Visit us today and experience the freedom of coffee!"
